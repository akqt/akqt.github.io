Built for continuity, innovation, and community impact

Officially launched in April 2026, LibraryOne was formed to preserve and advance the digital services once operated by Baker & Taylor. The company now delivers a unified ecosystem designed for public libraries that want modern access, dependable platforms, and strong patron engagement.

From discovery and acquisition to mobile reading and data-informed collection planning, LibraryOne supports every step of the library experience.

Solutions that serve librarians and patrons alike

Each product in the LibraryOne portfolio is shaped to solve a distinct need, from discovery and access to data-driven decisions and community outreach.

Boundless

A digital reading app and library platform that gives patrons mobile and web access to eBooks, audiobooks, and digital periodicals.

TitleSource360

A collection development and marketplace tool for discovering, evaluating, and acquiring new materials with confidence.

ePopUp Library

A QR-code-based access program that brings curated digital reading materials into schools, transit spaces, and community locations.

Content Café

A metadata enrichment tool that improves catalog discoverability and enhances results across library databases.

CollectConnect

A backend analytics engine that helps administrators track usage and align funding decisions with evidence-based collection planning.
